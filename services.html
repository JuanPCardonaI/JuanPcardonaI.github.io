SERVICES

The Cambridge Public Library is proud to offer an array of innovative services that reach beyond Research and Circulation.
  • Meeting Rooms for Cambridge based non-profit organizations
  • Delivery service for homebound patrons
  • Literacy programs including ESL, citizenship and basic computer classes
  • Assistive technology
  • Museum passes
  • Tax assistance
  • And much more!
The Cambridge Public Library will provide auxiliary aids and services, written materials in alternative formats, and reasonable modifications in policies and procedures to persons with disabilities, upon request.
